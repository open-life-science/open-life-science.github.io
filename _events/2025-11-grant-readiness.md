description: |
   The aim of the workshop is to help participants gain a better understanding of the funding landscape and the process of becoming “grant-ready.” Throughout the session, we will sketch a mental model of how the grant preparing process works, and identify useful resources to support grant preparation. The practical component of the workshop focuses on interpreting impact requirements from different types of funding calls, and practicing giving and receiving feedback on draft proposals. The workshop will be recorded, and all materials will be shared with the eLife Ambassadors community.

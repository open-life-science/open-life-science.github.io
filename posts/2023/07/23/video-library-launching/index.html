Launching the Open Seeds Video Library

Jul 23, 2023 - Bérénice

Mike Bird

Since its launch in 2020, OLS ran 7 cohorts, 279 cohort calls with 314 talks by 124 speakers. The cohort calls are all available on OLS YouTube channel and linked in the dedicated cohort schedule. But all these talks were also interesting individually and are currently hard to find.

To improve talk findability and give more visibility to these experts, we created the Open Seeds Video Library. This page shows the full library of available videos from talks in Open Seeds cohort calls: 314 videos by 124 speakers, organized by topic (Open Science, Tooling for Collaboration, Project, Community & Personal Management, etc), annotated with the recording date, information about the speakers, and slidedeck link when available.

The library is automatically created from the information listed in the schedule for each cohort, which is updated every week. In order to make this Open Education Material more FAIR, all videos, as the cohort calls in the schedule pages, are annotated with Bioschemas training profiles, and will be listed on ELIXIR TeSS (Training eSupport System) catalog.
